about

Hi there, I’m Corrigan. I’m a designer devoted to thoughtful packaging, laidback interiors, intuitive websites, and all things Martha Stewart. My designs lean towards the minimal and clean and are always 100% custom. Be it for a living room, a business card, or a landing page, I believe inspiration can come from anything — studio art, vintage media, old travels, architecture, etc.

In college I spent four years living and working in historic Charleston, S.C. and I could not get enough of it. The thorough preservation of the city’s architecture, retail, and hospitality lends a look into hundreds of years of Southern design. Charleston’s colors, silhouettes, and textures exhibit a classic, old-school feel that greatly influences my work.

After graduating with a degree in business I continued my work in interior design, mostly in the schematic design process. I fell in love with the process of getting to plan concepts for the look and feel of all things branding, web and interior design….especially when they’re used together in harmony to create a truly one-of-a-kind experience.
